# AI

## Trim AI-generated text before sharing

❌ DON'T: paste a raw AI-generated PR description, doc, or message without
editing it.

✅ DO: trim, rewrite, and remove fluff before hitting send.

Writing with AI is great. Dumping AI output on your teammates is not. Nobody
wants to review a wall of verbose, repetitive text that clearly wasn't
proofread.

A massive AI-generated PR description is one of the reasons people avoid
reviewing a PR — no human wants to read through that much filler.

Respect the reader's time: cut the slop, keep what matters.
